<objective>
**Plan 02-04: Batch Fee Slip Generation + Secondary Alert Escalation**

This plan delivers two capabilities: (1) batch fee slip generation with auto-numbered slips and batch mark-as-paid, and (2) secondary escalation logic for unresolved alerts — first alert triggers department head notification at 3 days, then leadership notification at 8 days.

**Purpose:** Enable administrators to efficiently process multiple fee payments in bulk with proper voucher tracking. Ensure unresolved fee alerts automatically escalate through the org chain so overdue fees are visible to management.

**Outputs:**
- FeeSlipNumberGenerator (Redis INCR, pattern FEE-YYYYMMDD-XXX)
- FeeRecordMapper.batchMarkAsPaid() with UpdateWrapper
- FeeRecordController POST /api/fees/batch-pay endpoint
- AlertEscalationTask (daily 5 AM, 3-tier escalation state machine)
- Escalation notification with proper RBAC user query (dept head -> leadership)
- BatchPayDialog.vue with selection, slip preview, payment date, voucher No
- NOTE: slip columns (slip_no, slip_generated_time, slip_generated_by) already exist in fee_record from V11 (created in 02-01) -- no additional migration needed
</objective>
